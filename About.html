The Evolution of Black Hole Discovery

Black holes were first predicted in 1916 from Karl Schwarzschild’s solution to Einstein’s general relativity. Initially theoretical, they became real with discoveries like Cygnus X-1 and the Event Horizon Telescope’s 2019 image.

“Black holes are where God divided by zero.” – Anonymous Astrophysicist

These objects reshape our understanding of space, time, and the limits of physics itself.
